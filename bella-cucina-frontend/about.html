OUR STORY & PASSION

Our Story

Bella Cucina was founded in 2000 by Chef Marco Rossi, who brought his family's authentic Italian recipes from Tuscany to San Francisco. What started as a small trattoria has grown into one of the city's most beloved Italian restaurants, serving over 50,000 happy customers and winning 12 prestigious culinary awards over 25 years.

Our commitment to authenticity means using only the finest imported Italian ingredients, preparing fresh pasta daily, and maintaining the traditional cooking methods passed down through generations. Every dish tells a story of Italian heritage and culinary passion.
